# Housing
A multifaceted concept encompassing the availability, affordability, quality, and security of living spaces, as well as the social dynamics within neighborhoods, with a focus on ensuring housing is accessible, meets basic human needs, and supports a sense of community, while recognizing housing as a fundamental human right.

## Housing accessibility
The availability of safe, suitable housing options for individuals and families, including factors such as location, availability of affordable housing, and the physical suitability of housing for diverse needs.

## Quality of public housing
The strengths and constraints of public housing, including long waiting lists and inadequate support for the disabled community.

## Cost of housing
The affordability (or lack thereof) of housing as experienced by residents due to rising housing costs within the city.

## Housing as a human right
The belief and principle that every individual is entitled to access safe, secure, and affordable housing, recognizing it as fundamental to dignity, well-being, and the ability to fully participate in society."
